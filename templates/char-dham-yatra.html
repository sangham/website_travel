Char Dham Yatra

Embark on a spiritual journey to the four sacred abodes in the Himalayas

The Four Sacred Dhams

Discover the spiritual significance of each pilgrimage site

Yamunotri

The source of the Yamuna River and the seat of the goddess Yamuna. It is the first stop in the Char Dham Yatra circuit.

  • Altitude: 3,293 meters
  • Best Time: May to October
  • Main Temple: Yamunotri Temple
  • Significance: Source of Yamuna River

Gangotri

The origin of the holy Ganges River, where Goddess Ganga descended to earth. It is the second destination in the pilgrimage.

  • Altitude: 3,100 meters
  • Best Time: May to October
  • Main Temple: Gangotri Temple
  • Significance: Origin of Ganga River

Kedarnath

One of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, situated near the Mandakini River. It is the third destination in the Char Dham Yatra.

  • Altitude: 3,583 meters
  • Best Time: May to October
  • Main Temple: Kedarnath Temple
  • Significance: Jyotirlinga of Shiva

Badrinath

Dedicated to Lord Vishnu, this temple is situated along the banks of the Alaknanda River. It is the final destination of the Char Dham Yatra.

  • Altitude: 3,133 meters
  • Best Time: May to October
  • Main Temple: Badrinath Temple
  • Significance: Abode of Lord Vishnu

12-Day Char Dham Yatra Itinerary

Our carefully crafted itinerary ensures a comfortable and spiritually fulfilling journey

Day 1: Arrival in Haridwar

Arrive at Haridwar, check into hotel. Evening Ganga Aarti at Har Ki Pauri. Overnight stay in Haridwar.

Day 2: Haridwar to Barkot

Morning drive to Barkot via Mussoorie. Enroute visit Kempty Falls. Check into hotel in Barkot. Overnight stay.

Day 3: Barkot to Yamunotri and Return

Early morning drive to Janki Chatti. Trek to Yamunotri (6 km). Darshan, pooja and return to Barkot. Overnight stay.

Day 4: Barkot to Uttarkashi

Morning drive to Uttarkashi. Visit Vishwanath Temple. Check into hotel. Overnight stay in Uttarkashi.

Day 5: Uttarkashi to Gangotri and Return

Early morning drive to Gangotri. Darshan and pooja at Gangotri Temple. Return to Uttarkashi. Overnight stay.

Day 6: Uttarkashi to Guptkashi

Morning drive to Guptkashi. Enroute enjoy beautiful views of Himalayas. Check into hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 7: Guptkashi to Kedarnath

Morning drive to Gaurikund. Trek to Kedarnath (16 km). Darshan and pooja at Kedarnath Temple. Overnight stay.

Day 8: Kedarnath to Guptkashi

Morning darshan at Kedarnath Temple. Afternoon trek down to Gaurikund. Drive to Guptkashi. Overnight stay.

Day 9: Guptkashi to Badrinath

Morning drive to Badrinath via Joshimath. Check into hotel. Evening darshan at Badrinath Temple. Overnight stay.

Day 10: Badrinath to Rudraprayag

Morning darshan at Badrinath Temple. Visit Mana Village. Afternoon drive to Rudraprayag. Overnight stay.

Day 11: Rudraprayag to Rishikesh

Morning drive to Rishikesh. Visit Triveni Ghat and Laxman Jhula. Check into hotel. Overnight stay.

Day 12: Rishikesh to Haridwar Departure

Morning visit to Rishikesh temples. Afternoon drive to Haridwar for departure with spiritual blessings.
